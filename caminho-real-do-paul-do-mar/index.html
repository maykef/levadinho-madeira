Book it. PR19 on SIMplifica

Once it shows open, book your €4.50 slot on SIMplifica in advance. Under-12s and residents are free but must still be named on the booking. PR19 is paid on its own separate booking and not included in the multi-day passes — check the live status above before you pay.

It's a point-to-point walk, so it doesn't finish where you parked — sort your return first (two cars, a pre-booked taxi, or a guided walk with transfers).

It's a long mountain drive from the coast, with no shop or reliable signal at most trailheads. Bring water, warm and waterproof layers, and a torch — the weather turns fast up high.

Closed or full? Your options

If IFCN closes PR19 (weather, landslip, works) or you can't get a slot: reschedule via the SIMplifica call centre before your date — you can move date/time freely, and switch trail only if yours was officially closed. For open alternatives, check the live board.

Before you go

  • Booking-only. Walking a paid PR trail without a valid SIMplifica ticket is an infraction, with fines reported up to €250.
  • It doesn't loop back. You finish somewhere else — fix your return before you set off.
  • Check the live badge the morning you go. Mountain trails close fast for weather, rockfall or works.
